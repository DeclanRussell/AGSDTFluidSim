Solver function that will call our solver kernal to calculate the new positions of the particles in.

our fluid simulation.

Parameters
_stream- the cuda stream we wish this kernal to run on
d_posArray- pointer to our gpu buffer that holds the postions of our particles
d_velArray- pointer to our gpu buffer that holds the velocities of our particles
d_cellOccArray- pointer to our gpu buffer that holds the cell occupancy count of our hash table
d_cellIndxArray- pointer to our gpu buffer that holds the cell index's of our particles
_hashTableSize- the size of our hash table. This is used to calculate how many blocks we need to launch our kernal with
_hashResolution- resolution of our hash table
_maxNumThreads- the maximum nuber of threads we need to launch per block
_smoothingLength- smoothing length of our simulation. Can also be thought of as cell size.
_timeStep- the timestep that we want to increment our particles positions in our solver
_particleMass- the mass of each particle. Defaults to 1.
_restDensity- the density of each particle at rest. Defaults to 1.
_gasConstant- the gas constant of our fluid. Used for calculating pressure. Defaults to 1.
_visCoef- the coeficient of viscosity in our fluid simulation. Defaults to 1.
_velCorrection- velocity correction using our XSPH method. Helps with compression, defaults to 0.3.
_densKernConst- constant part of the density kernal. Faster to compute once on CPU and load in.
_pressKernConst- constant part of the pressure kernal. Faster to compute once on CPU and load in.
_viscKernConst- constant part of the viscosity kernal. Faster to compute once on CPU and load in.
